---+!! Gravatar plugin

<img class="foswikiRight foswikiImage" src="%ATTACHURL%/example.jpg" />

%SHORTDESCRIPTION%

Add http://www.libravatar.org/ or http://gravatar.com user images to your foswiki.

When this plugin is enabled, Any Links to user's home pages get that user's gravatar image added to it.
